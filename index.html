About Us

Emma's Story

Emma grew up in a Christian family and knew about God from childhood. But at 13, she began asking deeper questions about identity, purpose, and who God really is. Seeing the genuine joy in her parents' lives, she searched for answers and found them in a heartfelt conversation with her father. When he shared Jeremiah 29:11, it changed everything. Emma discovered that a real relationship with God brings true joy and meaning — and she developed a passion to share that joy with others.

Constantin's Story

Constantin was also raised in a Christian home, where faith was part of daily life. Yet as he grew older, he questioned whether his faith would truly become his own. That changed when he joined a home church connected with Campus Crusade for Christ. There he encountered authentic Christian community and experienced God personally. He dedicated his life to Christ, was baptized alongside his brother and sister, and began sharing the Gospel with others.

Our Family

Emma and Constantin met through this ministry, united by a shared passion to serve God. They got married seven years ago and now serve together in Moldova. They are blessed with three children: Samuel, Rebecca, and Lea. As full-time staff with Cru Moldova, they are committed to planting and multiplying churches, helping others discover the same joy and purpose in Christ.

Our Ministry

We invest in the next generation, make disciples, and multiply churches across Moldova.

Reach

We engage young people through English Clubs, summer camps, and outreach events — building friendships and sharing the Gospel through genuine relationship.

Grow

We walk alongside new believers through small groups and one-on-one mentorship, helping them study God's Word and become committed disciples who live out their faith.

Multiply

We launch youth ministries in new cities and empower leaders to plant home churches. Our vision: one fellowship for every 1,000 people across Moldova.
